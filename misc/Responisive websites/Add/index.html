Meet Larry

Larry is our transcriber here at Newman Industries. Larry is able to operate a computer like any regular person. He can send emails and follow voice commands. Larry uses advanced computer programming. Larry types at speeds of 120wpm on average.

Meet X03

X03 is an advanced driving robot. X03 uses visuals from the passemger along with motion detection software to predict and navigate the roads. X03 is so impressive that he might be getting his license soon

AeVo12

Aevo12 is the most advanced learning robot in the world. AeVo12 can learn any skill within a matter of minutes. At Newman Industries, AeVo12 is responsible for monitoring, diagnosing and fixing the Newman servers.

The Cyonic Arm

The latest in our inventions, the Cyonic Arm. The Cyonic arm is a bio-engineered arm created to help disabled people. The arm is water resistant, shock resistant and can easily lift 100 pounds. It is controlled by either by voice activation from the user or muscle tension movements. The arm can detect slight muscle changes and through learning patterns, predict what the user whats to do. Though still in the works, the Cyonic arm is looking like the next big thing.
